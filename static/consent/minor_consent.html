Students' PATHS Study

People have different reasons for doing things, and they learn differently. We would like to learn more about your experiences and motivations. Your answers will be used to inform programs for young people like yourself.

Completing this online program is voluntary and your grade in this class will not be affected. Try to complete all of the activities and questions. If you do not want to complete an activity or answer a question, you may skip it. You also may stop participating at any time. There are no wrong answers and you will not receive a grade based on your performance on these activities.

Your participation is confidential and no information about you or your school will be identified in any published report. Responses and results are private, and your teacher will not have access to the answers you give.

Please complete this online activity by yourself, without the assistance of teachers, classmates, or others.
