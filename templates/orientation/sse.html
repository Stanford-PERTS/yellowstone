The Goal: Support Students, Improve Outcomes

Well-designed learning environments can help students make sense of challenges in ways that bolster academic performance and foster well-being. Research shows that:

  • Students’ experiences of their learning environments can critically affect their academic outcomes
  • Learning environments attuned to students’ experiences can help more students persist through challenges

Understand Students’ Experiences

The Survey of Student Experiences (SSE) is a free, easy-to-use tool that helps colleges and universities better understand how their undergraduate students experience their campus learning environments and strategically adopt evidence-based resources to help more students succeed, particularly those from backgrounds traditionally disadvantaged in higher education.

The Survey of Student Experiences helps institutions

  • Better understand their students’ experiences, focusing on pivotal moments and beliefs that can affect well-being, levels of engagement, and academic outcomes
  • Identify areas in which their students may benefit from additional support
  • Select the most relevant evidence-based resources to help motivate and support their students
