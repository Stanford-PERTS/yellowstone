New Teacher Center (NTC), EDENS Lab, and PERTS are collaborating on a pilot program to provide support for building empathy in student-teacher relationships. Research shows that student-teacher relationships are important for student success. In a recent peer-reviewed study, suspension rates dropped by 50% in the classes of teachers who completed a 60-minute training about the importance of supportive student-teacher relationships (Okonofua, Paunesku, & Walton, 2016; see also “Don’t Suspend Students. Empathize.”) These dramatic results underscore what many teachers know and what research has shown time and again: students engage and learn best when they feel trusted, respected, and valued by their teachers.

The Classroom Connections Program (CCP) is a data-driven professional learning experience designed to help teachers build strong, empathic relationships that provide students with the trust, respect, and psychological safety that are essential to academic engagement and success. The CCP includes an empathic mindset training developed by Dr. Jason Okonofua and colleagues. The training will be delivered in the context of the New Teacher Center's proven coaching model, giving teachers the support they need to adapt the empathic teaching strategies with their own students.

Finally, the PERTS Copilot platform will provide teachers and coaches with rapid formative feedback about the effects of the training on studentsâ€™ classroom experiences, relationships, and engagement. Teachers will work with coaches to complete online lessons and implement three improvement cycles, each including brief student surveys, collaborative reflection/planning with coaches during meetings, implementation of plans, coach observation, and refinement. We anticipate these activities will be highly valuable to instruction and in providing data-driven guidance to the field.

About PERTS

PERTS is an applied research center based out of Stanford University. We believe that evidence-based strategies should benefit all students, not just the privileged few. To make research accessible to all, we empower educators everywhere to implement evidence-based strategies cost-effectively. We are so excited to bring you Growth Mindset for College Students and to make the latest research on growth mindset strategies actionable to every college in the United States.
