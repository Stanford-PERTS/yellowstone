Transition Mindsets Privacy Notice

Our Privacy Notice is fueled by our commitment to the following Privacy Principles:

  1. We’re deeply committed to creating a safe and secure online environment for our users.
  2. We do not store any personal information. We only store anonymized survey responses.
  3. We do not sell the anonymized survey responses to third parties.
  4. We take the protection of data very seriously.
  5. We do not advertise. We use survey responses to provide users with a better learning experience, not to sell products.

About Transition Mindsets and Our Privacy Notice

Transition Mindsets feels strongly about protecting the privacy of our users. We are committed to creating a safe and secure environment for our users. This Privacy Notice describes how the Transition Mindsets team collects and uses the information users provide on our Properties. We use the data we collect to provide users with a better experience.

Our Privacy Notice describes what information we gather from our users and others who visit and use our Properties, how we use that information, and what we do to protect it. By visiting or using our Properties, users consent to the information handling practices described in this Privacy Notice.

How We Collect Data

Personal Information.

We do not store any “personally identifiable information” or “personal data” (such as full name, email address, or phone number) that can be used to identify users. We do ask users to provide the first three letters of their first name and the first three letters of their last name so that we may link their two online sessions. However, we protect this data with a one-way encryption method so it is impossible to reconstruct the original information or connect it to any individual.

Information about the use of our Properties and other user-provided information.

We will collect usage information about the use of certain features on our Properties, as well as users’ responses to survey questions. This enables us to improve the experience for our users.

Location Information.

We will collect and use information about a user’s location (such as country). Users may change the settings on their computer to prevent it from providing us with any location information.

How We Use Data

To enhance our Properties and the services we provide.

Transition Mindsets uses the information users provide or that we collect to enhance the experiences of our users. For example, Transition Mindsets remembers a user’s recent activity so if the user needs to complete a session at a different time, when the user logs in again, it will pick-up where he or she last left off.

To understand how Users use our Properties.

Transition Mindsets uses all of the information that users provide or that we collect to understand and analyze the usage trends, learning behaviors, and preferences of our users, to improve the way the Properties work and look, and to create new features and functionality.

Data Retention

Unless we receive a deletion request, we will retain users’ information.

Technical Information

To provide a high-quality experience for our users, we may use various technologies that automatically record certain technical information from the browser or device, including standard log files, web beacons, or pixel tags. This technical information may include the Internet Protocol (IP) address, device or browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring or exit pages, clickstream data, operating system, and the dates and times that users visit the Website. We do this to better understand how our users are using our Website so we can improve site functionality and the services we offer.

Like most websites, we may send one or more cookies—small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters—to users’ computers. Cookies remember information about users’ activities on a website and enable us to provide users with a better experience. Transition Mindsets may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears automatically after a user closes a browser. A persistent cookie remains after a browser is closed and may be used by the browser on subsequent visits to the Website. A user can, however, remove a persistent cookie at any time. Please review the web browser “Help” file to learn the proper way to modify cookie settings. However, without cookies users will not have access to certain services and features on the Website.

Transition Mindsets uses a variety of third-party service providers, such as YouTube to host our videos or Google Analytics to understand our Website usage. We may allow third-party service providers to place and read their own cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information through the Website. This information is collected directly and automatically by these third parties, and Transition Mindsets does not participate in these data transmissions. Transitions Mindsets will, wherever feasible, contractually obligate our third-party service providers to abide by the terms of this Privacy Notice.

How We Share or Transfer Data

Transition Mindsets takes great care to protect the information users provide us. We do not rent or sell data that we collect from users with third parties.

We may share anonymous or aggregate data to improve our services and learn more about our users.

In certain occasions, Transition Mindsets may work with partners to improve our services or offerings. We may disclose automatically collected and other aggregate non-Personal Information to authorized partners to conduct research or assist in understanding the usage, viewing, and demographic patterns for certain programs, content, services, and/or functionality on our Properties.

We will share data when required by law.

Transition Mindsets may also disclose User information if required to do so by law, or if we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with local, state, federal, international, or other applicable laws (such as U.S. Copyright law) or respond to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena, or warrant, or administrative request. In some cases, we may make such disclosures without first providing notice to users.

We may share data in the context of a change of business, including a merger or acquisition.

In the event that Transition Mindsets is acquired by or merged with a third-party entity, we may transfer or assign the information that we have collected from users as part of such merger, acquisition, sale, or other change of control.

We may share data when necessary or appropriate to protect Transition Mindsets or others.

Transition Mindsets may disclose information that we believe, in good faith, is appropriate or necessary to take precautions against liability; to protect Transition Mindsets from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful uses; to investigate and defend ourselves against any third-party claims or allegations; to assist government enforcement agencies; to protect the security or integrity of our Properties; or to protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Transition Mindsets, our users, or others.

Our approach to Data Security

Data security is extremely important to us.

To protect our users’ privacy and security, we do not collect any personally identifiable information” or “personal data” (such as full name, email address, or phone number) that can be used to identify them. We only ask users to provide the first three letters of their first name and the first three letters of their last name so that we may link their two online sessions. We use a one-way encryption method so that this information is never actually stored. It is only used to match a user’s Session 1 data with the same user’s Session 2 data.

We try to ensure that our Properties and information sent to us are safe, but no security measures are perfect.

Transition Mindsets uses certain physical, managerial, and technical safeguards designed to preserve the integrity and security of users’ survey responses and other information we maintain in connection with our Properties. We cannot, however, ensure or warrant the security of any or all of the information users transmit to Transition Mindsets, and users do so at their own risk. Once we receive transmission of information, Transition Mindsets makes commercially reasonable efforts to ensure the security of our systems. When users enter sensitive information, we encrypt the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL) or similar technologies. However, please note that this is not a guarantee that such information may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.

Links to other sites

The Properties may link to and may be linked by websites operated by other entities or individuals. This Privacy Notice does not apply to, and we cannot always control the activities of, such other third-party websites. Users should consult the respective privacy policies of those third-party websites.

International visitors

Our Properties are operated and managed on servers located within the United States. If users choose to use our Properties from the European Union or other regions of the world with laws governing data collection and use that differ from U.S. law, then they acknowledge and agree that they are transferring their information outside of those regions to the United States and that, by providing their information on the Properties, they consent to that transfer.

Changes and updates to this Privacy Notice

Transition Mindsets may modify or revise this Privacy Notice from time to time. Transition Mindsets will notify users of any changes to our Privacy Notice by posting the revised Privacy Notice with an updated date of revision on our Properties.
